How do I cancel an order?

Our Petal Scarves place great emphasis on orders received by customers. Therefore, we will process each order as soon as possible to satisfy customers.

Once the order has been placed, we will send the details of the order made together with the confirmation email for the customer to confirm the order.

If the customer wants to cancel the order, the customer is advised to immediately call our service line at 03-54822244 within 24 hours after the order has been placed.

If payment has been made, we will ask you to refill the details and account number to make it easier for us to make a refund to the customer.

If this notification is not made within the relevant period, we will process the order and the payment will not be refunded.
